Dufford & Brown, P.C. has ceased operations

For over 50 years, Dufford & Brown has been part our Colorado community, serving as not just your lawyers, but your confidants and advisers.  Sadly, August 15, 2016 was the last business day for Dufford & Brown. However, all of our former attorneys will continue to offer the services you have relied upon at their new firms. We will be communicating directly with all of our present and former clients on the administrative matters relating to the firm’s closure and invite you to contact us with any questions you may have.

It has been our honor to serve you, and we look forward to continuing to do so in our new practices.

If you are looking for any of our former attorneys, you can find their contact information on this page or search for them on the Colorado Bar Association.
